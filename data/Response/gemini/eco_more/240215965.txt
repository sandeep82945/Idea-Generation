1. **Developing a comprehensive intelligent logistics information system for CBEC:** This system would integrate data from various sources, such as customs, shipping companies, and warehouses, to provide real-time visibility into the movement of goods across borders. This would help to improve efficiency and reduce costs.
2. **Exploring the use of blockchain technology to enhance the security and transparency of CBEC transactions:** Blockchain could be used to create a secure and tamper-proof record of all transactions, which would help to reduce fraud and build trust between buyers and sellers.
3. **Investigating the potential of artificial intelligence (AI) to automate tasks in CBEC logistics:** AI could be used to automate tasks such as order processing, inventory management, and customer service. This would help to improve efficiency and reduce costs.
4. **Developing new strategies for managing returns and exchanges in CBEC:** Returns and exchanges are a major challenge for CBEC businesses. New strategies are needed to make this process more efficient and cost-effective.
5. **Conducting research on the environmental impact of CBEC:** CBEC can have a significant environmental impact, due to the transportation of goods across borders. Research is needed to identify ways to reduce this impact.
